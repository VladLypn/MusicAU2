How it works

Every chord has a purpose.

MusicAU hides repetition inside a game loop. Instead of drilling the same shape in isolation, players learn what each chord does and use it under pressure.

Competitive practice

Sharpen your guitar skills, then prove them.

MusicAU is more than a solo practice tool. Players can compete through chord battles, score races, seasonal ladders, and boss challenges where cleaner playing creates stronger moves.

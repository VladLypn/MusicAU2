Market and launch

A large guitar audience, starting with focused local validation.

Stage 1 - Free demo

Guitar battles, 3-5 learning levels, and a frictionless entry point. The goal is proving retention before monetization with 50,000 registered users, 10,000 MAU, and 25% D7 retention.

~1M new guitarists every year
~50M active guitar players
~700M people play or have tried guitar

Stage 2 - Freemium

Battles and basic levels stay free. A EUR4.99/month subscription unlocks learning tools, progress tracking, and harder levels, targeting 30,000 MAU, 3% paid conversion, and EUR4,491 MRR.

Go-to-market

Start with a local Cyprus launch, music school pilots, beginner testers, and teacher feedback. Then expand through Reddit, TikTok content, and user-generated challenges.
